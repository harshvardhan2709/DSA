Basic Rules of Tennis

  • A match is played as best of 3 or 5 sets.
  • Players must serve diagonally across the court.
  • Points go as: 15, 30, 40, and game.
  • A tie-break occurs at 6–6 in sets.
  • A ball on the line is considered in.
